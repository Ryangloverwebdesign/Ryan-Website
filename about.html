About Lillian Vucic

Background & Story

Lillian originally set out to build a career in media, working alongside well-known figures in the industry. While she valued the experience, she quickly realized she craved more autonomy and freedom—something corporate jobs couldn’t offer.

A natural people person, Lillian has a gift for connecting with others and delivering exceptional results through patience, insight, and compassion. She’s committed to offering outstanding service to everyone she works with.

With her close family involved in real estate investing and property ownership, Lillian decided to make the leap into the industry herself. She started paving her way as an independent real estate agent and has never looked back.

Currently

Lillian has been a real estate agent for 8 years now and has helped 200+ families with rentals, sales and buying. She looks forward every day to keep on working and providing the best service for her clients in Queens, New York.

Lillian is an energetic person who loves to solve problems and communicate with people so she can help out and understand them better. Her clients appreciate her genuine character, trustworthiness, reliability, and willingness to work with them every step of the way.

Her personalized approach to helping clients sets her services apart. Her willingness to understand what is that you want, her genuine willingness to help you out is how Lillian will help you achieve your goals.
