How long does it approximately take to sell my property with you?

There are two main factors that determine whether your property gets sold quickly or in a few months, those being:

- Price
- Condition

Pricing and condition ultimately decide how fast your home will get sold for. If the home is too expensive for what's it's actually worth then it will take longer to sell, but if you price the home too low it can quickly sell. If the home's condition is great and the price is set fairly then this helps selling the home faster.

On average it takes us to sell:
- Regular House: 30 to 60 days to close
- Co op: around 90 days to close
‍
This is why we make sure to find a perfectly balanced price based on our Comparative Market Analysis and on your homes condition, all to guarantee you get the best deal possible.
