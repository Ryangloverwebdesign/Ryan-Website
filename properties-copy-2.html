About the Building

This Co-op is located in Rego Park, which is a neighborhood in the borough of Queens in New York City.

Rego Park has a strong residential feel with a mix of single-family homes, co-ops, and apartment buildings, making it a popular spot for families and young professionals. It’s conveniently located near several subway lines, including the M and R trains, which provide easy access to Manhattan. Rego Park is also known for its commercial strip along Queens Boulevard, which hosts the popular Rego Center, a large shopping complex with major retail stores and dining options.
