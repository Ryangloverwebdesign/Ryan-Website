Property Description

Welcome to this House-sized 2 bed / 2 bath apartment with terrace in The Lafayette – a pre-war building in Forest Hills. The apartment is within a few blocks walk to the E/F/N/R, LIRR (15 minutes to Grand Central), 3 express bus lines as well as shopping, year-round farmers market and the world-renown West Side Tennis Club. Zoned for P.S. 303 Elementary which is ranked #3 in NYC.

This gracious apartment has high ceilings, 9 windows to let in abundant light, as well as 7 closets! In addition, the apartment retains its original pre-war charm with parquet floors, archways between the rooms and original doors and hardware.

The large kitchen boasts 2 full size windows for cross-ventilation, stainless steel appliances, large countertop area and lots of cabinets as well as a washer/dryer. Attached to the kitchen is a large dining area for seamless entertainment. Both bathrooms were fully renovated in 2022.

The Lafayette is a pet-friendly, full-service Co-op building with a live-in Super, part-time doormen and a great staff. There is a Fitness center ($25/month), bike room ($25/year per bike), newly renovated laundry room, and garage with a wait list. Maintenance includes water, heat and cooking gas. No flip tax, 25% down payment, guarantors permitted.
