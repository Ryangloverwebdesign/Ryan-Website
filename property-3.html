Property Description

Welcome to Acropolis Gardens! This lovely One bedroom, One bath Co-op is situated in the heart of Astoria.

About the Building

This Co-op in Ditmars-Steinway is located in Queens, New York City, known for its rich cultural diversity and historic charm. Named after the Steinway & Sons piano factory established there in the 19th century, this area retains a close-knit, residential feel with tree-lined streets, low-rise buildings, and vibrant communities.

Ditmars Boulevard is the main commercial hub, featuring a mix of local shops, cafes, and restaurants with a wide variety of international cuisines. The neighborhood has a family-friendly atmosphere with parks, such as Astoria Park along the East River, offering scenic views and recreational activities. It’s also well-connected by the subway, making it a popular choice for people who enjoy a quieter yet convenient lifestyle within NYC.
