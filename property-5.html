Property Description

This Lovely Studio is located in the Coop Park Plaza building located in Rego Park. The studio features has a spacious layout. It has hard wood floors through out the apartment and a private balcony. It is located on the 12th Floor.

The kitchen is an open concept design and the the living area has a spacious amount of space. It has three main closets in the apartment. The Kitchen has a gas range stove, refrigerator and microwave. The bathroom has a full shower and tub, and vanity.

The apartment has been recently been Renovated with upgraded clean finishes. The building features a doorman, elevator, laundry room. It is a short walk to Costco, Marshalls in the Rego Center for shopping. A short walk to 63rd Drive Train Station ( M, R)lines and Q 38 bus. Coop board approval applies. Small pets ok.
